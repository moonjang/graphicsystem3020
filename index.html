Type 2023 This typography exhibition shows the design outcomes from the course, Graphic Systems in the graphic design area, Lamar Dodd School of Art, University of Georgia. The project, Type Investigations is designed to understand type characters as a visual system, to raise a sense of various type expressions, and to explore fundamental and innovative typestyles.
